About the game

The object of the game is to control the flight of a bee that is constantly moving between rows of green pipes. When they encounter them, the game ends. The control is carried out by pressing buttons, in which the bee makes a small jerk up. In the absence of jerks, the bee falls due to gravity, and the game will also be completed. Points are earned for each successful flight between two tubes. The gameplay does not change during the game.
